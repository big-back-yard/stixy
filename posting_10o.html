 Tall building like this have a problem with sway. The answer is to have a big counterweight inside that reduces the building sway. In Taipei 101 is a 728 ton ball, painted gold and on display.     
 

 

 
 But it's not enough to have a 728 ton spherical tuned mass damper. You've got to make it fun for kids.

How do you make a 728 ton spherical steel tuned mass damper fun? Well, how about you create a cute cartoon character and toys based on it. You call them uhhh, ummm ...

 28 Oct 2012 And naturally I had to take some time to visit the computer/electronics store district. I did buy some things but I found it not so convenient. It's organized as a huge number of independent shops many selling the same thing so finding what you wanted involved a lot of leg work. A little like the old "computer shows" we used to go to. And comparison shopping even more a workout. I did not think the pricing was much better than if you shopped carefully in the states. I guess you have to do your due diligence anywhere you shop.
