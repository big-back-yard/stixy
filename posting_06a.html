 One problem we had was that it was especially crowded. Normally bicycle traffic has one side of the bridge, pedestrians the other. But on our day the bike side was closed for maintenance work so everyone used one side.
 

 

 
 You can probably tell it's a bit (or a lot) contrast enhanced but this is a picture of the sunset from the bridge.

I don't have the picture I took of the young couple we met on the the way back because I used their camera. He had just proposed to her on the bridge and it was the first photo of them after getting engaged. I congratulated them both. Now whenever they see this famous bridge they will be reminded of that day. I  hope it remains a happy memory for them; it's not always so in this crazy world.
